# Race and ethnicity codes and labels used by the U.S. Census Bureau

Race and ethnicity codes and labels used by the U.S. Census Bureau

## Usage

``` r
RACE_ETHNICITY_SUBTABLES
```

## Format

A tibble with ten rows and five columns:

- Census Race:

  A short description of a race or ethnicity

- Suffix:

  A capital letter designating a race or ethnicity

- Inclusive Table:

  The name of a table that includes people of single and multi-racial
  identities that claim this identity

- non-Hispanic:

  The row in table `B03002` with counts of people with this racial
  identity and no Hispanic ethnic identity

- Hispanic:

  The row in table `B03002` with counts of people with this racial
  identity and a Hispanic ethnic identity
