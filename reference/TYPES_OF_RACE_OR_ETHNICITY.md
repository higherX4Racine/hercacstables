# For selected tables, an alphabetic suffix follows to indicate that a table is repeated for the nine major race and Hispanic or Latino groups:

For selected tables, an alphabetic suffix follows to indicate that a
table is repeated for the nine major race and Hispanic or Latino groups:

## Usage

``` r
TYPES_OF_RACE_OR_ETHNICITY
```

## Format

### TYPES_OF_RACE_OR_ETHNICITY

A data frame with 9 rows and 2 columns.

- Code:

  `<chr>` A one-letter suffix

- Race or Ethnic Group:

  `<chr>` A racial or ethnic identity such as "Black Alone" or "Hispanic
  or Latino".
